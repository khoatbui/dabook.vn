About DABOOK.VN

DABOOK.VN is a trademark of Dai Minh Trading, Service and Tourism Company Limited, established in April 2004 in Hanoi.

The company is proud to serve travelers and investors in the last 15 years, and aiming to become the leading Destination Management Company in Vietnam, continuing the mission to provide quality services: domestic and international air tickets; package tours, tailor-made tours; domestic and international golf tours; providing visa services; travel insurance; etc.

We have perfected the technical infrastructure to best serve our customers on the basis of digital applications-Online service.

The core value of the company lies in Reputation, Connection and Technology, to help customers save time and lower cost. We understand that customers now need: fast, accurate, convenient, secured services as well as various forms of ticket purchase, ticket delivery, and payment.

We are proud to be a member of IATA, International Air Transport Association.

We are proud to be enlisted in the top agents of Asiana airlines, Korean air and Vietnam Airlines' routes between Korea and Vietnam for 15 consecutive years. We have been selected as the nest agency of Bamboo Airway in 2019 and we will continue to provide the best service to our customers.

We are pleased to be the agent providing Air tickets and Travel services for more than 3000 regular customers and organizations such as Lotte hotel, Lotte E&C, Hyundai Aluminum, Samsung Welstory, Korean Embassy in Hanoi, Korean International School in Hanoi, Dongyang E&C and more.

The company has a Ticket Office in My Dinh, Vietnam with 25 dynamic employees who serve 24/7 and hotline available at any time. Our ticket delivery staffs are also eager to serve you. We seek to provide accurate consultancy and dedicated guidance to our customers.

Dai Minh Travel Company is based in My Dinh, Hanoi. You can also find our tour desk at Lotte Hotel in Lieu Giai, a 5-star hotel. For a clear and professional operation, we receive legal and investment consultancy from T&H Law Firm.
